Meet me

I am a PhD student in Mayra Vidal's lab at the University of Massachusetts, Boston. I use field, laboratory, and genomic studies to better understand the diet breadth evolution in insect herbivores. I use public art and outreach to try and foster a familial realtionship among my human and non-human neighboors. I am a NSF Graduate Research Fellow and an alum of Georgetown University.
